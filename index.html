SimpliLife Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

SimpliLife ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal daily life tracker for habits, tasks, stats, and reflections. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information We Collect

To provide the app's core functionality, we collect and store the following, linked to your account:

  • Email address — used to create and sign in to your account.
  • Account identifier — a unique user ID associated with your account.
  • Content you create — your habits, daily tasks, tracked stats, and reflections.
  • Profile photo — only if you choose to add one.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the app: to authenticate you, save your data so it syncs across sessions, and display your habits, tasks, stats, and reflections back to you. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

How Your Data Is Stored

Data is stored securely using Supabase, our backend and database provider, and transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing. Data is processed only by infrastructure providers (such as Supabase) strictly to provide the service.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below, after which it will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

SimpliLife is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
